The Showdown Effect - Tournament 2 - About

The Showdown Effect Tournament 2 will be streamed live on twitch at twitch.tv/giuseppeiii3 with recordings later posted to youtube at youtube.com/user/GiuseppeIII3.

Each team will consist of three members, balanced to be kept at similar skill level.

Sign up to be considered to become a part of a team. Players of all skill and experience levels welcome and encouraged!

Each match will consist of three best of seven games, with the first team to win two best of 7 games moving on to the next round, and the losing team being given a placement or going to a different part of the bracket as outlined here.

All characters are available in matches except Thelma Vayne who has been banned from tournament play through community vote.

All stages are available in matches, including Fort Fiesta which was banned in tournament one through community vote. The stage for the first game will be choosen via random generator, the stage for the second game will be chosen by the loser of the first game, and the stage for the third game (if necessary) will be Battle of Bandits.

All weapons are available in matches, however there will be a weapon pick and ban system in place as follows:

In the first and second games of a match, both teams will simultaneously:

1. Ban a weapon combination,
2. Pick a weapon combination,
3. Ban a weapon combination,
4. Pick a weapon combination,
5. Ban a weapon combination,
6. Pick a weapon combination

While in the third match, both teams will simultaneously:

1. Pick a weapon combination,
2. Ban a weapon combination,
3. Ban a weapon combination,
4. Pick a weapon combination,
5. Ban a weapon combination,
6. Pick a weapon combination

When a weapon combination is banned it can not be used by any member of the opposing team, when a weapon combination is picked, it can be used by only a single member of team. However, until a weapon combination has been banned by the opposing team, it can be picked multiple times by a team.

Exploits, such as, but not limited to sword whirlwind or intentionally crashing the game are banned in tournament play. Any bad faith attempt to circumnavigate these rules or engage in other acts of unfair play may result in automatic match losses or outright disqualification depending on the severity of the action, as determined on a case-by-case basis.

If you wish to promote, stream, or in some other way assist with this tournament please contact me on The Showdown Effect Discord @GiuseppeIII. Any other questions, comments, suggestions, etc. about the tournament of The Showdown Effect can also be asked and are welcomed there!

Best of luck to all players and teams!
